How Engagement Drives Outcomes

The more a student uses the app, the more likely they are to advance onto a target pathway.

How engagement predicts pathway enrollment
Students grouped by milestones completed: Low is 1 to 3, Mid is 4 to 7, High is 8 or more. Each bar shows the share who advanced onto a target pathway.
Students grouped by how many milestones they have completed.

What this means: Students who complete 8 or more milestones advance onto a target pathway 93% of the time, nearly triple the 36% among students who complete just a few. Every milestone a student finishes is linked to a stronger outcome.

The Four Data Layers

Each layer builds on the last, giving the city a fuller picture of its cohort over time.

LAYER 01
Engagement Data
● Live
Every student's unique in-app fingerprint — session cadence, screen flow, matching preferences. Establishes the "before" state that makes every future outcome provably causal.
LAYER 02
Milestone Progression
● Live · MMF Phase 1
30 structured, student-reported checkpoints across the High School, Post-Secondary, and Early-Career pathways — each one visible to the student's mentor to sharpen guidance.
LAYER 03
Outcome Attribution
● Live
Causal link between mentorship activity and real outcomes — target-field enrollment, credential completion, field-match, first role, advanced credentialing.

One digital career home for the city's existing programs

P3 does not replace what Lake Charles already runs. It connects it: each program becomes a tracked pathway on the platform, every participant can pick up a mentor, and every outcome rolls up to this dashboard.

Recreation & Parks youth programs
Summer camps and sports leagues gain a career layer: every participant can pick up a mentor and a pathway.
Community Development workforce initiatives
Existing workforce and neighborhood programs get digital enrollment, tracking, and outcome reporting.
Partner school career programs
Career days and counselor caseloads become year-round pathways with milestones instead of one-off events.
Local employer internships
Employer placements plug into the funnel, with every internship tracked from application to hire.

The innovation story: the city gets a single, live view of career readiness across every district and every program it already funds, the kind of economic-development instrumentation most cities cannot buy at any price, at the cost of a single program line item.
